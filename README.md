# U.S.-Medical-Insurance-Costs
For this portfolio project, I am investigating a medical insurance costs dataset in a .csv file using Python skills I have developed.

# Goals of the Project
The variables of the dataset include age, sex, BMI (body mass index), children, smoking (yes/no), region, and medical charges. I decided to examine the average age of patients, analyze where the majority of individuals are from, figure out what the average age is for someone who has at least one child in the dataset, and look at different costs between whether the individual is a smoker or non-smoker.
